Our Story

Real Thai flavors,
no shortcuts

Aroi (อร่อย) means delicious in Thai — and that's the one promise we make with every plate. We bring the bold, fragrant, and vibrant tastes of Thailand straight to the streets of southern Minnesota.

From our pad thai to our curries, every dish is prepared fresh with traditional recipes and real ingredients. We believe great food shouldn't be complicated to find — just look for the truck.

What We Serve

Fresh & Flavorful

Classic Thai dishes made with care, every order cooked to order.

🍜
Pad Thai
Stir-fried rice noodles with egg, bean sprouts, green onion, and your choice of protein — finished with crushed peanuts and lime.
🥢
Pad See Ew
Wide rice noodles wok-tossed with soy, egg, and fresh vegetables. Smoky, savory, and deeply satisfying.
🍚
Thai Fried Rice
Wok-tossed jasmine rice with egg, vegetables, and your choice of protein — light, savory, and satisfying.
🍊
Orange Chicken
Crispy chicken tossed in a bright, tangy-sweet orange sauce. A crowd favorite that's hard to resist.
🌿
Pad Kee Mao
Drunken noodles — wide rice noodles stir-fried with Thai basil, chilies, and your choice of protein. Bold and aromatic.
🧋
Boba Tea
Creamy, sweet, and refreshingly cold with chewy tapioca pearls. The perfect companion to any dish.
